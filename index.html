How to play

Controls

  • Click a soldier (on the board or its card) to select it. Reachable tiles light up.
  • Click a tile to move (costs action points), or an enemy in a red reticle to fire.
  • Click an arrow in the ring around a soldier to turn it on the spot.
  • Click a soldier's grenade button, then a tile in range, to lob a grenade — click it again or press Esc to cancel.
  • Hover any soldier to see the tiles it can watch. Walls give cover — the hit % drops.
  • Leave a soldier facing the right way with spare action points and it fires on enemies who cross its view next turn.
  • Doors start closed — open one next to a soldier, then step through. Windows are see-through, but smash them (up close, or by shooting) before you can fire through.
  • A grenade cooks — it goes off at the end of the turn, so clear the orange blast zone. A blast blows open breakable (cracked) walls and can leave an impassable crater.
  • Drag the battlefield to scroll; arrow keys or WASD also pan.
