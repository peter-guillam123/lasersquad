How to play
  • Click a soldier to select. Highlighted tiles are within reach.
  • Click a tile to move (costs action points), or an enemy in a red reticle to fire.
  • Click an arrow in the ring around a selected soldier to turn it on the spot.
  • Hover any soldier to see the tiles it can watch. Walls give cover — the hit % drops.
  • Blue tiles let you move and still keep enough AP to react next turn; grey tiles spend you out.
  • Leave a soldier facing the right way with spare AP and it fires on enemies who cross its view.
  • Doors start closed — click one next to your soldier to open it, then click it to step into the doorway, or click the ✕ badge to close it. Windows are see-through, but smash them (click up close, or shoot) before you can fire through.
  • End your turn to hand the screen to the other player.
